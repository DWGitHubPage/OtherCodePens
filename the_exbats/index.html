T he Exbats, started by daughter/father duo Inez and Ken from Bisbee, Arizona. Inez drums and sings, Ken plays guitar. They sound like if the kids from the movie Over the Edge had listened to the Ramones and decided to start a band instead of locking their parents up inside a school and trying to burn it all down. They make catchy, fun songs about things like Xena, how cool their mom is, and being a witch with a silly sense of humor. They're originally from Portland, OR. but moved to Arizona more than a few years ago. They've put out two albums and one compilation album, as well some ep's and singles. They met Burger Records through Matt Rendon of the Resonars, who also runs Midtown Studios where the Exbats have recorded some of their stuff. Rendon also plays bass on some of their recorded output.
